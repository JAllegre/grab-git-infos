This extension respects user privacy and data security. It only accesses information displayed on GitHub and GitLab issue pages opened by the user. All data is processed locally within the browser and is neither collected, stored, nor transmitted to third-party services. The extension does not track browsing activity outside of the pages required for its functionality. No personally identifiable information is collected or used. Users retain full control over the extension and can disable or uninstall it at any time.
